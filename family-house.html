Project family-house

An old thing becomes new if you detach it from what usually surrounds it.

We used this quote in the following project. Goal was to breathe life into the old-fashioned house and to make it more spacious and brighter because original composition did not meet the criteria of current trends of living. We accomplished it by tearing down the wall between kitchen and living room. Space for a terrace was created.
